Historical Monuments of India

Taj Mahal

A symbol of love, the Taj Mahal in Agra is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world, located in Delhi, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture.

Red Fort

A historic fort in Delhi, symbolizing the Mughal Empire's grandeur and India's independence.

Hawa Mahal

The "Palace of Winds" in Jaipur, famous for its unique facade with 953 windows.

Gateway of India

A monumental arch in Mumbai, built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary.
